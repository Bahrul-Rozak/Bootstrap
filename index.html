About Us

RMD Steel is a manufacturer specializing in stainless steel products, catering to industries such as kitchen, healthcare, and pharmacy. Although we are a newer player in the market, RMD Steel is committed to delivering innovation and high quality in every product we offer. We specialize in the production of various stainless steel items, from commercial kitchen equipment to medical supplies that meet safety and hygiene standards. Our team consists of experienced professionals who provide tailored design and production solutions to meet the specific needs of our clients. RMD Steel also offers curving and custom fabrication services, ensuring that every product we produce can fulfill the unique requirements of our customers. We prioritize sustainability and efficiency in our manufacturing processes while maintaining high quality standards. With the motto "Reliable Quality for Every Need," RMD Steel is ready to be your trusted partner in fulfilling your innovative and high-quality stainless steel product requirements.
